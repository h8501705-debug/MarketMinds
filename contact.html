📊 About PulseQuant

PulseQuant is a free, lightweight stock chart viewing platform built specifically for Indian traders and investors. No registration, no hidden costs — just clean, fast charts to help with your market analysis.

Powered by TradingView charts and featuring a database of over 7,000 Indian stocks, PulseQuant gives you professional-grade tools right in your browser. All your watchlists are stored locally, so your data stays private.
