📊 About MarketMinds

MarketMinds is a free, lightweight stock chart viewing platform built specifically for Indian traders and investors. No registration, no hidden costs — just powerful tools to help you make better trading decisions.

Powered by TradingView charts and featuring a database of over 7,000 Indian stocks, MarketMinds gives you professional-grade analysis tools right in your browser. All your watchlists are stored locally, so your data stays private.
